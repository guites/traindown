Transponder

Simply record your training.

No subscriptions, no lock in, no fragile interfaces. Own your training data by using the Traindown Transponder to record your sessions with the Traindown markup language. Traindown is a language to help athletes express their training that is inspired by Markdown.

Traindown Transponder is Open Source

All source code for Traindown Transponder is available on GitHub.

Need help?

Any questions? Do you have the app and need some help? Please send an email to tyler at greaterscott dot com. Also consider filing an issue on the GitHub page linked above.

Privacy Policy

Traindown Transponder collects zero personal information. All data never leaves your device other than when you email it from within the app. There are zero analytics collected from either the app or this site.

In the future, there may be a data sync feature added to the app. If that should occur, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect that. The app itself will always remain free and free of collecting your data. Should the sync service exist, there will be a separate service that will require data such as your email to access the syncing feature. The Traindown Transponder will never itself collect any personal information.
